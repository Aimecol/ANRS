Children's Nutrition Recommendations

Get age-appropriate nutrition recommendations for your child based on weeks, months, or years of age. Our guidance is tailored to support healthy growth and development using locally available Rwandan foods.

Enter Your Child's Age

We'll provide nutrition recommendations based on your child's developmental stage.

Why Age Matters

Children's nutritional needs change dramatically as they grow. From exclusive breastfeeding in early infancy to a varied diet for school-age children, each stage requires specific nutrients in appropriate amounts.

Our recommendations follow World Health Organization (WHO) guidelines adapted for locally available Rwandan foods.

Nutrition Through the Ages

Enter your child's age to receive specific recommendations. Here's a brief overview of key developmental stages:

0-6 Months

Exclusive breastfeeding is recommended, providing all necessary nutrients for optimal growth.

6-12 Months

Introduction of complementary foods while continuing breastfeeding, focusing on iron-rich foods.

1-5 Years

Transition to family foods with balanced nutrition to support rapid growth and development.
